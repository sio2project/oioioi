You do not like fiddling with pendrives and CD-ROMs in the mail, do you? We don't, too.

This is why you can use this simplified procedure: upload your solution using the form below. You will get back a PDF document, which you send to us in a regular postal mail. It will take us a second to scan the code embedded in the document and your submissions will be judged.

You can generate many such documents, but do not send more than one per task. If we receive from you multiple documents for a single task, we will pick up one randomly.

If for some reason you would like to use the standard procedure, with CD-ROMs or pendrives, you still can.
